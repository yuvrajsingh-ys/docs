<Check>
  ### **You can migrate**

  - All message templates that were created on other platform
  - All the catalogues that were created while using other platforms.
  - All the Whatsapp forms that were created while using other platforms.
  - Blue tick, verified name and conversation limit.
</Check>

<Warning>
  ### **You cannot migrate**

  - Your campaigns and broadcast that were created on other platforms.
  - Your chat flows that were created on other platforms.
</Warning>

## **Steps to Migrate Whatsapp Account to  Convertalk**

 1. Login to your registered Meta Account.
 2. Under "Accounts" click on "Whatsapp Accounts".
 3. Select the Whatsapp account you want to migrate into  Convertalk.
 4. Then click on "Whatsapp Manager". It will be available in the bottom of the account information.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 1 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-2.png)
 5. Once clicked on "Whatsapp Manager". Go to settings and then go to Account tools in it.
 6. In "Phone Numbers", click on settings button for the phone number you want to migrate and select "Two-Step Verification".
 7. Now click on "Turn off two-step verification". You will receive an email to disable two-factor authentication.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 4 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-3.png)
 8. Login to your  Convertalk Portal and Click the "Create Bot" button on the top right.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 4 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-4.png)
 9. Select "WhatsApp" as the platform on the next page.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 5 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-5.png)
10. Provide the required details and follow the bot creation process.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 6 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-6.png)
11. Scan the QR Code and initiate a WhatsApp message to create the bot.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 7 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-7.png)
12. Upon successful verification, Click "Connect to your WhatsApp number" to start the Automated onboarding.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 8 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-8.png)
13. Select all the pre-requisite and click "Proceed"

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 9 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-9.png)
14. Choose Automated or Manual onboarding. In this document, We will talking about Automated onboard.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 10 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-10.png)
15. It will open up a new pop-up window where you have to complete the Meta onboarding.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 11 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-11.png)

    <Info>
      Please make sure that you have not blocked the pop-up window in your browser. If yes, please enable the same.
    </Info>
    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 12 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-12.png)
16. Now click on "Get Started" to continue connecting your account to  Convertalk.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 13 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-13.png)
17. Now as a first step, fill your business information. Select the business portfolio which you want to connect with from the drop down. All your portfolios will be available in the drop down.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 14 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-14.png)
18. Then enter a business name, website or profile link and select a country. Then click on "Next".

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 15 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-15.png)
19. Now from the drop down to select a WA business account choose "Create a WhatsApp Business Account" and also select "Create a new WhatsApp Business profile". And click on "Next".

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 16 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-16.png)
20. Now enter Whatsapp business account name and WA business display name. Then select the category of your business. And click on "Next".

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 17 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-17.png)
21. Now enter the phone number with country code that you want to add for Whatsapp. After that select a method you want to use to verify your number. You will receive an OTP. Here we selected Text Message.
22. After clicking on "Next" a pop up will be opened to enter the OTP received.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 18 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-18.png)

    <Info>
      Once the OTP is entered and your number is verified your new whatsapp bot will be successfully created.
    </Info>
    <Info>
      Note: In this process you will be asked to enter the OTP received on your registered phone number. Remember not to enter the OTP and hold this process.
    </Info>
    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 19 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-19.png)
23. Update Payment Method to WhatsApp

In the Business Manager, update the payment method for your WhatsApp Business Cloud API account to enable seamless transaction processing.

Access WhatsApp Account Settings

In the "Meta Business Manager," locate and click on the "Payment Setting" button for your WhatsApp Account.

![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 20 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-20.png)

24. Navigate to Billing and Payments Page
25. On the "Billing and Payments" page, click on the "Add Payment Method" option to add a new payment method.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 21 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-21.png)
26. Choose the payment method type that you want to use for your WhatsApp account.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 22 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-22.png)
27. Enter the necessary payment details based on the selected payment method.

    ![229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 23 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-23.png)
28. After completing the payment details you can go back and enter the received OTP that was asked earlier.

    <img
      src="/images/platform-guidelines-training/migration-of-account/229.migration_of_whatsapp_account_from_other_platforms-1.png"
      alt="229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 1 Pn"
      title="229 Migration Of Whatsapp Account From Other Platforms 1 Pn"
      style={{ width:"80%" }}
    />
29. Once you have entered the OTP. Click on Finish, and your bot will be migrated to  Convertalk.

    <Info>
      If OTP is expired and new OTP is not received, wait for 72 hrs before requesting a new OTP.
    </Info>

Done\! You have migrated your WhatsApp Bot into  Convertalk.
