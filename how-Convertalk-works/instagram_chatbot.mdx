### **Why Instagram Chatbot?**

Creating an Instagram chatbot offers several benefits that can significantly enhance your online presence and business operations:

- **24/7 Customer Support:** You can respond instantly to customer inquiries at any time of the day.
- **Increased Engagement**: Chatbots can engage with your audience in real time, sending personalized messages, updates, and promotions.
- **Lead Generation**: By interacting with visitors and taking essential information, chatbots can help generate leads, making it easier to convert potential customers into actual customers.
- **Scalability**: Unlike human agents, chatbots can handle multiple conversations simultaneously.

To create your Instagram chatbot and manage all your chatbots, you first need to visit the  Convertalk Dashboard

### **How to Create Your Instagram Chatbot?**

Follow these quick steps to create your Instagram chatbot:-

1. To start creating your new chatbot, click **"Create New Bot."**

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-1.png)

2. You can view all the bots you have created in the left sidebar.
3. Select the primary purpose for creating your bot and proceed further.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-2.png)

4. Click on "Instagram Chatbot" to begin creating your chatbot.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-3.png)

5. Click on “Login” and enter your login credentials.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-4.png)

6. Enter your phone number/email address and click **“Log in”.** If you want to create a new Facebook account, click on **“Create New Account”**

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-5.png)

7. Now, click on **“Get Started”. **Make sure you have an Instagram account.

   ![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-6.png)
8. After logging in with your Instagram account, you’ll see the below screen.
9. Select the **Business account** to start your Instagram Chatbot and click “Next”.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-7.png)

10. Review your business contact information and complete your remaining details.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 9 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-8.png)

11. Click on “Save” to continue further.

![8 Instagram Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 9 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/8.instagram_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-9.png)

**You have created your Instagram chatbot\!**
