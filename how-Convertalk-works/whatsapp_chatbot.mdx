Here is the process to create the WhatsApp Bot using the  Convertalk's Automated onboarding.

This document will guide you through the step-by-step process of creating the  Convertalk WhatsApp Bot using the WhatsApp Business Cloud API. Following these instructions, you can set up your WhatsApp Bot and integrate it with the  Convertalk platform.

## **Step 1: Check for Pre-requisites**

Before you begin, ensure you have the necessary prerequisites in place, such as access to the  Convertalk platform, WhatsApp number, etc.

**Pre-requisites**

Before you start creating the WhatsApp bot, Make sure that you have

- A phone number that is
  - Active & capable of receiving SMS.
  - Not registered on WhatsApp or WA Business App.
- Registered legal business
- An active website with a valid privacy policy
- Business use case compliant with [WhatsApp](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/commerce-policy) and [Meta commerce policy](https://www.facebook.com/policies_center/commerce/).
- A valid payment method (Debit or Credit card) capable of making international payments to Meta for conversation charges.
- GSTIN Number, Only if the business is registered in India.

If you have all of the above checked, You are good to start with WhatsApp Cloud API Bot.

<Note>
  Please note that a single mobile number can only be utilized on one of the WhatsApp services at a given time, namely WhatsApp, WA Business, or WhatsApp Business API. If you wish to use your existing number, you must remove your account from one service before using it on another.
</Note>

### **Step 2: Create a WhatsApp bot & go through the WhatsApp Automated onboarding process**

#### **2.1 Register your bot on  Convertalk**

1. Login to your  Convertalk Portal and Click the "Create Bot" button on the top right.

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-11.png)
2. Select "WhatsApp" as the platform on the next page.

   Choose Platform

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 12 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-12.png)
3. Provide the required details and follow the bot creation process.

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-5.png)
4. Scan the QR Code and initiate a WhatsApp message to create the demo bot 1st. Scan or Send Confirmation message

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 6 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-6.png)
5. Upon successful verification, Click "Connect to your WhatsApp number" to start the Automated onboarding.

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-7.png)

#### **2.2 Complete Meta Automated onboarding**

1. Select all the pre-requisite and click "Proceed"

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot Convertalk Resource Centre 8 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-8.png)
2. Choose Automated or Manual onboarding. In this document, We will talking about Automated onboard.

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 9 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-9.png)
3. It will open up a new pop-up window where you have to complete the Meta onboarding.

   ![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 10 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-10.png)

   <Note>
     Please make sure that you have not blocked the pop-up window in your browser, If yes, Please enable the same.
   </Note>
4. Upon successful completion, Your bot will be connected to WhatsApp. But as a last step, You have to update your payment method.

### **Step 3: Update Payment Method to WhatsApp**

In the Business Manager, update the payment method for your WhatsApp Business Cloud API account to enable seamless transaction processing.

**3.1 Access WhatsApp Account Settings**

In the "Meta Business Manager," locate and click on the "Payment Setting" button for your WhatsApp Account.

![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-1.png)

**3.2 Navigate to Billing and Payments Page**

On the "Billing and Payments" page, click on the "Add Payment Method" option to add a new payment method.

![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-2.png)

**3.3 Select Payment Method Type**

Choose the payment method type that you want to use for your WhatsApp account. Select Payment Method Type

![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-3.png)

**3.4 Enter Payment Details**

Enter the necessary payment details based on the selected payment method. Enter Payment Details

![7 Whatsapp Chatbot  Convertalk Resource Centre 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/7.whatsapp_chatbot__Convertalk_resource_centre-4.png)

**3.5 Verify Payment Method**

Once you have entered the payment details, proceed with the verification process.

**3.6 Update Business Information**

1. After successful verification, make any necessary updates to your business information.
2. You have successfully updated the payment method on your WhatsApp account.

Congratulations\! You have successfully created the  Convertalk WhatsApp Bot using the WhatsApp Business Cloud API and integrated it with the  Convertalk platform. Your WhatsApp Bot is now ready to interact with users and provide automated responses.

<Info>
  Please note that specific steps and requirements may vary based on updates and changes in the  Convertalk and WhatsApp Business Cloud API platforms. If you encounter any difficulties during the process or have further questions, please refer to the official documentation of  Convertalk and WhatsApp Business Cloud API, or contact their support teams for assistance.
</Info>
