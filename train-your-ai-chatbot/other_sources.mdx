### **How to train your AI Chatbot?**

To manage all your chatbots, you first need to visit the  Convertalk Dashboard

To manage your bot, select it from the left sidebar.

![28 Other Sources  Convertalk Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/other-sources/28.other_sources__Convertalk_resource_centre-1.png)

Click on **“Train Your AI Chat Bot”** and start training your AI Chat Bot.

![28 Other Sources  Convertalk Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/other-sources/28.other_sources__Convertalk_resource_centre-2.png)

#### **Other Sources**

1. You can connect your Notion Page and your chatbot will retrieve information from it.
