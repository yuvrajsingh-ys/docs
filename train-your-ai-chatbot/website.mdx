### **Why train on Websites?**

Training bots on websites helps them understand your content better so they can answer user questions more accurately. It saves time by using your existing website data and ensures your chatbot gives responses that match your brand and information. This makes support faster, smarter, and more helpful for visitors.

---

### **How to train your AI Chatbot on Website?**

1. Log into your  Convertalk, and choose the bot you wish to train.

   ![23 Website  Convertalk Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/website/23.website__Convertalk_resource_centre-1.png)
2. Click on **“Train Your AI Chat Bot”** and start training your AI Chat Bot.

   ![23 Website  Convertalk Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/website/23.website__Convertalk_resource_centre-2.png)
3. Click on the "**Website**' tab to add the website.

   ![23 Website  Convertalk Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/website/23.website__Convertalk_resource_centre-3.png)
4. Enter the URL of the website. You can train the website using 3 ways:
   - Scan all pages: Scans the current page, and all the pages linked to the website.
   - Scan Single Page: Scans and trains only on current URL.
   - Scan JSON information: Only scans the JSON information.

     <Info>
       You can also click on the "Advance option" to train the bot on images as well. Not that training on images takes significantly more time and cost than normal training.
     </Info>
5. Once done, click on the "**Save**" button. The website will start training.

   ![23 Website  Convertalk Resource Centre 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/website/23.website__Convertalk_resource_centre-4.png)

Congratulations\! You have trained your bot on a website\!

### **FAQ's:**

Here are some common FAQ's regarding website training:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is my bot not training from selected web pages?">
    Make sure you have a valid URL, and give time for the website to train on.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How to retrain your bot on a site?">
    Click on the circular arrows to retrain or "Sync" your website.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is the bot not answering information that is present on my site?">
    make sure the information is present in a clear format. Information that is in unique fonts may have trouble being trained on. Additionally, if information was in photos or images, then make sure to train the website on images as well, under the "Advance option".
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My site is freezing when I enter the URL">
    This is happening because the URL being entered is invalid. Kindly enter a valid URL. We are also working on this issue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the method of training your bot on questions that are not being answered?">
    Train these questions on the unanswered qusetions tab.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
