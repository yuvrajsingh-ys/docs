### To setup your WhatsApp Templates

1. Navigate to your WhatsApp Bot.

2. Click on **WhatsApp Automation**.

3. Select **Templates**.

4. You will get an option to select pre-defined Templates. You can either select from **Chatflow Template** or **Message Templates**.

---

#### **CHATFLOW TEMPLATE**

5. When you hover your mouse on any template, you will get the option to preview or use the template.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-1.png)

6. While previewing, you can click on **"Use Template"** to directly use it in your chatflow.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-2.png)

7. Once you click **"Use Template"**, your chatflow template will be ready to use and edit inside **"Edit Your Chat Flow"**. You can customize it according to your requirements.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-3.png)

---

#### **MESSAGE TEMPLATE**

8. Click on **Message Templates** to select a pre-defined template from the **"Templates Gallery"**, or create and customize your own template by selecting **"Your Templates"**.

9. When you hover on any pre-defined template, you can either preview it first or directly click **"Use Template"**.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-4.png)

---

### **With Regular Template**

10. You can create a new template in the **"Your Templates"** section by selecting **"Create Template"**.

11. Under **Create Templates**, enter a name/title and category for your template, and select a language. You can also select a type for your template from the dropdown.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-5.png)

12. To configure your template, select a format for the header from the dropdown and enter the header text you want to display to your customer. Alternatively, you can select a media file to share — it can be an image, video, or document.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 6 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-6.png)

13. Enter the content for your template inside the **Body** box. You can use variables in the body and configure them later.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-7.png)

14. Enter the content for the **Footer** that will be displayed in your template.

15. You can also add **Buttons** to your template. After selecting the button type from the dropdown, configure them as required.

16. Click **"Save as Draft"** if you want to make changes later, or click **"Submit for Approval"** once you are done creating the template.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 8 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup__Convertalk_resource_centr-8)

17. In **"Manage Templates"**, your template will be visible. It will get approved by WhatsApp once submitted for approval.

18. Under **Actions**, you have options to copy the template ID, delete the template, and select a flow for the buttons inside the template.

19. Similarly, you can configure flows for your template replies using the buttons added to it.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 8 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-9.png)

---

### **With Carousel Template**

20. When you select **"Carousel"** as the Template Type, you will get the following options:

21. **Bubble Text** — Add text for the template and include variables.

22. **Carousel Setup** — Set up cards, configure their media, and add CTA buttons.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 10 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-10.png)

23. Inside **"Bubble Text"**, enter your text and click **"Add Variable"** to insert a variable.

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 12 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-11.png)

24. Inside **"Carousel Setup"**, select a button type — either **"Call to Action"** or **"Quick Reply"** — and fill in the required details accordingly.

25. Create a card for your carousel. Add an image and a description.

26. Add a clickable button below the card.

<Note>
You can add up to 10 cards for your carousel.
</Note>

![38 Whatsapp Template Setup  Convertalk Resource Centr 12 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-template-setup/38.whatsapp_template_setup_\_Convertalk_resource_centr-12.png)

27. Click **"Save as Draft"** or **"Submit for Approval"**.

---

Done! You have created your WhatsApp Template. Now wait for it to be approved by Meta.
