The **Telegram Profile** section provides key information about your bot. This includes:

1. **Bot Name**
   - Displays the name of your bot.
2. **Bot Username**
   - Shows the unique username associated with your bot.
3. **Access Token**
   - Provides the bot's access token, essential for connecting and managing the bot.
