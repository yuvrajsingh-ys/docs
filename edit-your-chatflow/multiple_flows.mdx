## **How to create a trigger flow:**

1. **Create a New Flow**:
   - Select your website bot and navigate to the "Edit Your Chatflow" section.

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component Convertalk Resou 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-1.png)
   - Click on "Create New Flow" (as shown in the screenshot).

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component Convertalk Resou 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-2.png)
   - Now enter the "Flowname" and click on the "Create new flow".

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component  Convertalk Resou 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-3.png)
   - Configure this new chat flow . It will appear in the flow list on this page.

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component  Convertalk Resou 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-4.png)

   ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component  Convertalk Resou 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-5.png)
2. **Set Up Redirect in a Previous Flow**:
   - Go to the existing flow where you want to trigger the new flow.
   - Add the "Redirect" chat component (as shown in the screenshot).

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component  Convertalk Resou 6 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-6.png)
   - From the dropdown, select the flow you wish to redirect to.

     ![14 Multiple Flowsredirect Component  Convertalk Resou 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/multiple-flows/14.multiple_flowsredirect_component__Convertalk_resou-7.png)
3. **Flow Redirection**:
   - Once set, the bot will automatically redirect to the selected flow when the redirect component is triggered within the conversation.

This allows you to seamlessly connect multiple flows within one chatbot, enhancing the flexibility of your chat interactions on the website.
