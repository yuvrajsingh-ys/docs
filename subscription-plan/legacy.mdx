## Convertalk Subscriptions

Convertalk is a SaaS-based chatbot provider platform where you can create a website bot, Live chatbot widget, Chatbot page, WhatsApp bot, Telegram bot, and Facebook bot. Currently, you can manage your subscription to bots from the  Convertalk Dashboard, except for WhatsApp, which is handled offline. Here is how you can manage your subscriptions.

## **Current Subscription**

Check your current subscriptions from the `subscriptions` panel. You can access the same by clicking on the `Subscriptions` in the left side menu bar and then selecting the `Subscriptions` tab in the top menu bar as shown in the image below. Here you can see all of your current subscription details.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-2.png)

## **Upgrade your plan**

You can upgrade your plan in a few quick steps; here is how:

**Step 1**: Navigate to the `Subscriptions` panel from the left menu bar and then go to the `Plan` tab.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-3.png)

**Step 2**: Once you can see the screen similar to above,

a. Select whether you want to purchase the Monthly or yearly plan from the top left.

<Check>
  You will get two months FREE in the yearly plan.
</Check>

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-1.png)

b. Select the number of messages you want to consume monthly from the message slider bar.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-4.png)

c. Now click on `Upgrade` button as shown below in the image.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-5.png)

Once you click on the `Upgrade` button, You can Follow the on-screen instructions and make the payment. Once payment is made, You are good to go. In case of any issues contact us at [contact@ Convertalk.com](mailto:contact@ Convertalk.com) .

## **Downgrade/Cancel plan**

You can downgrade your plan in a few quick steps; here is how:

**Step 1**: Navigate to the `Subscriptions` panel from the left menu bar and then go to the `Plan` tab.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-6.png)

\*\*Step 2: \*\*Click on the `Free Plan` button available under the baby plan option.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-7.png)

**Step 3:** Once you click on the Free plan button, you will see the below pop-up, You can downgrade your plan by clicking on the `Downgrade to FREE plan`.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 8 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-8.png)

**Step 4:** Once you complete step 2, you will see the confirmation message that your cancellation/downgrade request has been placed. You need to confirm the same from the `Subscription` tab as shown in the image below. You should see the downgrade request as shown in the image below. If yes, You are done\! Your plan will be downgraded in next billing cycle and you won't be charged from now on.

![199 Subscription Plans Legacy Version  Convertalk Res 9 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/subscription-legacy/199.subscription__plans_legacy_version__Convertalk_res-9.png)
